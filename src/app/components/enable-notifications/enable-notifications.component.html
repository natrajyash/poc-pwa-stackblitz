Enable Notifications

Notifications could be disabled by the browser if you are in incognito mode

(OR)

You may have disabled the Notifications

Try following steps to re-enable notificaions (In Chrome)

  • go to chrome://settings/content/notifications
  • scroll down the Block list, containing all the websites that are blocked from emitting push notifications
  • delete localhost from the Block list
  • Reload the applcation and try again.
